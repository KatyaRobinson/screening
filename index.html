About La Locanda

For us, food is not just a way to our hearts, it is one of the joys of life itself

At La Locanda, our master Italian chefs take old world traditions and infuse them with new world style to offer a truly diverse menu. From Dover Sole, filleted table side to our succulent Rack of Lamb, we offer only the finest, most well-prepared Northern-Italian inspired dishes you will find.

With great food and the perfect setting for any occassion, we look forward to making your experience at La Locanda unforgettable.
